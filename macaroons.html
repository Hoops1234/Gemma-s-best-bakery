Macaroons

Macaroons are delicate, sweet meringue-based cookies made with almond flour, egg whites, and sugar.
They are known for their crisp shell, chewy interior, and vibrant colors, often filled with buttercream, ganache, or jam.
Perfect for any occasion, macaroons are a delightful treat enjoyed around the world.
